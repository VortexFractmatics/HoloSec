At HoloSec, we believe that privacy is not a feature; it is a fundamental human right. Our architecture is designed to be "decoupled," meaning we have no access to your data or your encryption keys.

1. DATA COLLECTION (ZERO FOOTPRINT)

The HoloSec engine runs locally on your machine. We do not transmit your files, your keys, or your keystrokes to any server. Your "Vault" is your own. If you lose your keys, we cannot recover them because we never had them.

2. THIRD-PARTY PROCESSING

We use Gumroad to process payments and manage our affiliate program. When you purchase a license, Gumroad collects your payment information according to their privacy policy. HoloSec only receives confirmation of purchase to issue your license seat.

3. ANALYTICS

Our website uses zero tracking cookies, zero Facebook pixels, and zero Google Analytics. We have no interest in tracking your digital movement. We only track successful license activations for seat-count compliance.

4. CONTACT

For transmissions regarding this protocol, contact: holosecurity@proton.me
