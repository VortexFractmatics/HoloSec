ID: 026 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The Grid Down Scenario

An Electromagnetic Pulse (EMP)—whether from a solar flare or a high-altitude detonation—would fry most electronics instantly. Your cloud data might still exist on a server in a bunker somewhere, but the internet to access it will be gone for years.

Local Survival

If the grid goes down, the only data you own is the data you can hold in your hand. HoloSec is optimized for portable, low-power storage (USB/SSD).

The Faraday Cage

We recommend storing your HoloSec backup drive inside a Faraday Bag (a shielded pouch that blocks all signals). This protects the drive from EMP spikes and remote wiping. When the lights go out, you will be one of the few people with access to your financial records, deeds, and survival manuals.
