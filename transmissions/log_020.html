Your Refrigerator is Watching You

ID: 020 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The IoT Nightmare

Smart TVs. Alexa. Ring Doorbells. Smart Thermostats. We have filled our homes with microphones and cameras that are constantly connected to the internet. These devices are notoriously insecure and often have default passwords.

The Network Bleed

If you keep your sensitive data on a Network Attached Storage (NAS) drive or your main PC connected to Wi-Fi, a compromised Smart TV can be used as a bridge to attack your files. Hackers pivot from the TV to the router to your PC.

The Air-Gap Necessity

HoloSec is the antidote to the Smart Home. It creates a "Digital Faraday Cage." By keeping your critical data (passwords, wills, deeds) on a HoloSec USB drive, you physically remove it from the network. Your toaster can hack your Wi-Fi all it wants; it cannot hack a USB stick sitting in your pocket.
