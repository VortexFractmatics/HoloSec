Your Copier Has a Hard Drive (and It's Full)

ID: 039 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The Forgotten Hard Drive

Every modern office copier/printer has a hard drive inside. It stores a digital image of every single document scanned, copied, or printed. When you sell that copier, you sell your company's secrets.

VoIP Eavesdropping

Your desk phone is a computer. If your network is breached, hackers can turn on the microphone remotely and listen to your boardroom meetings. This is standard industrial espionage.

The Digital Bunker

HoloSec advises "Compartmentalization." High-value IP should never touch the office network. It should never be printed on the networked copier. Keep it on a HoloSec encrypted drive, view it only on air-gapped screens, and share it only via physical handoff.
