Intel Management Engine (IME)

Did you know that modern Intel CPUs have a tiny, separate operating system running inside them (Minix)? It has full access to your memory and network, even when your computer is "off." Security researchers have long suspected this is a backdoor.

Trust Nothing but the Code

You cannot trust the hardware. You can only trust the math. HoloSec's encryption happens at the software layer, before the data hits the disk controller. Even if your CPU has a backdoor, it only sees the encrypted ciphertext, not the keys (which are flushed from RAM instantly after use).

Platform Agnostic

HoloSec works on Linux, Windows, and Tails OS. If you suspect your Windows machine is compromised by hardware bugs, you can boot into a Linux USB stick and open your HoloSec vault there. Your data is portable; your hardware risk is contained.
