HIPAA is Not Enough: Protecting Patient Data

ID: 012 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The Compliance Trap

Many private practice doctors and therapists believe that using a "HIPAA Compliant" cloud service makes them safe. It does not. It only makes them legally compliant. If that cloud provider gets hacked (like the Change Healthcare breach of 2024), your patients' most sensitive data is exposed.

Sovereign Patient Records

For high-profile clients (celebrities, politicians), standard cloud storage is a liability. Their records need to be off the grid entirely.

HoloSec allows you to create an Offline Patient Vault. This volume resides on an external drive that is only plugged in when you are actively updating a file. The rest of the time, it is air-gapped.

The "Need to Know" Architecture

With HoloSec, you can partition records. You can give your receptionist access to the scheduling volume (Shard A) but keep the clinical notes volume (Shard B) restricted to your biometric authentication only. This ensures that even if your office staff is compromised, the deep medical history remains sealed.
