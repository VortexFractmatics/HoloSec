Digital Pickpocketing: The RFID Threat

ID: 033 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The Walk-By Hack

Your credit cards and passport likely have RFID chips (the "tap to pay" symbol). A thief with a $20 scanner can walk past you in a subway or crowd, scan your pocket, and steal your card numbers or passport data instantly. No touch required.

Shielding Your Keys

This same vulnerability applies to hardware security keys (YubiKeys) if they are NFC-enabled. If you carry your digital keys in your pocket, they can be queried.

HoloSec Hardware Hygiene

HoloSec recommends storing your USB vault drive in a specific RF-shielded container (Faraday bag) when moving through high-density urban environments. We treat data storage as "Radio-Active"â€”it must be shielded at all times.
