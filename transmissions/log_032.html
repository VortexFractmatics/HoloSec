Machine Identification Code: The Invisible Yellow Dots

ID: 032 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

You Are Being Tracked on Paper

Think printing a document is safe? Think again. Almost every color laser printer sold today secretly prints a pattern of microscopic yellow dots on every single page. These dots encode the printer's serial number and the exact date/time of printing.

Whistleblowers Beware

Reality Winner (NSA leaker) was caught because the FBI scanned the leaked document, found the yellow dots, traced the serial number to a specific printer, and checked who used it at that time. Paper trails are digital trails.

The Digital Handover

HoloSec advises against physical printing for sensitive leaks. Instead, use our Steganography Module (Log 005) or Encrypted Shards (Log 006) to transport data digitally without creating a forensic paper trail.
