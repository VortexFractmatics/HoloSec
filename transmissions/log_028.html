SIM Swapping: Your Phone Number is Not a Key

ID: 028 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The 2FA Nightmare

You use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) via SMS text message. You think you are safe. You are not. A teenager can call T-Mobile, pretend to be you, and convince a minimum-wage support agent to "swap" your SIM card to a new phone.

Instant Takeover

Suddenly, your phone loses service. The hacker gets your 2FA texts. They reset your email password. They drain your Coinbase account. It takes 15 minutes.

Decoupling Identity from Telecom

HoloSec does not use SMS verification. We do not use email recovery. We use Cryptographic Keys. Your access is determined by what you know (your password) and what you have (your keyfile/shard). A hacker can steal your phone number, but they cannot steal the math in your head.
