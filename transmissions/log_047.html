HOW TO KILL A DRIVE

⚙

Formatting is not enough.

If you are decommissioning a drive that held sensitive data, you have two options:

1. Thermite: Melts the platters (Fun, but dangerous).

2. The Drill Press: Drill three holes through the physical platters. This shatters the glass/ceramic substrates and destroys the magnetic alignment.

If you use HoloSec, this physical destruction is just the final fail-safe. The data was already encrypted random noise. But we believe in overkill.
