The Invisible Fingerprint: Scrubbing Metadata

ID: 016 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

Data About Data

When you take a photo, your phone records the GPS coordinates, the time, and the device model. When you edit a Word doc, it records the author name and total edit time. This is "Metadata."

John McAfee was famously located and arrested because a journalist posted a photo of him that still contained GPS metadata in the EXIF headers. You can be tracked without ever typing your name.

The Automated Scrubber

HoloSec includes an automated Metadata Scrubber. When you drag a file into the secure vault, the engine strips this hidden data automatically.

  • Images: Removes EXIF, IPTC, and XMP data.
  • Documents: Removes Author, Company, and Edit History.

The file inside the vault is "clean." If you decide to leak it or share it later, it cannot be traced back to your device. It is a digital ghost.
