The 10-Year Archive Rule

ID: 058 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

Hard Drives Rot.

If you put a hard drive in a safe today, in 10 years the grease in the bearings will freeze or the magnetic bits will "flip" (Bit Rot). Flash memory leaks charge over time.

The Migration Mandate:

  • Every 5 years: Migrate data to new hardware.
  • Every 10 years: Migrate to a new algorithm (AES -> HoloSec Lattice).

Data is not a rock. It is a living thing. If you do not feed it (power it up, verify checksums), it dies. HoloSec includes an "Auto-Verify" integrity check to warn you before your data rots.
