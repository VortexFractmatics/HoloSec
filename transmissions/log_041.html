The Spy in Your Toolbar: Browser Extensions

ID: 041 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

"Read and Change All Data on Websites"

Check your browser extensions. Coupon finders, grammar checkers, dark mode enablers. Almost all of them request permission to "Read and change all data on all websites you visit."

The Data Heist

This means the extension can read your bank balance, your medical portal, and your emails. Many free extensions sell this "clickstream data" to data brokers. Some are sold to malware groups who push a silent update that turns the extension into a keylogger.

The Clean Browser Policy

HoloSec advocates for a "Zero Extension" policy on any machine used for secure tasks. Do not mix convenience with security. If you need to access your HoloSec vault, do it on a machine that has no third-party code running inside the browser.
