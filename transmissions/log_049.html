The Clean Network: Why You Need a Pi-hole

ID: 050 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

Blocking the Trackers at the Door

Ad-blockers in your browser are good, but they miss things. Smart TVs, phones, and IoT fridges bypass browser extensions. You need network-level filtering (like a Pi-hole or NextDNS) to block tracking domains before they even load.

The DNS Sinkhole

These tools act as a "Sinkhole," discarding requests to known tracking servers. This speeds up your network and protects your privacy.

HoloSec: The Data Sinkhole

While Pi-hole protects your traffic, HoloSec protects your storage. They are two halves of the same shield. Use DNS filtering to stop them from watching you online. Use HoloSec to stop them from seeing what you save offline. Together, you disappear from the grid.
