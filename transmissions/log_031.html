BlueBorne: Why Your Bluetooth Should Be Off

ID: 031 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The Airborne Virus

Most people leave Bluetooth on 24/7 for their headphones or smartwatch. This is a mistake. Vulnerabilities like "BlueBorne" allow hackers to take control of your device without you even pairing with them. They just need to be within 30 feet of you.

Silent Exfiltration

Once connected, they can siphon contacts, photos, and keystrokes. You will see no prompt. You will hear no alert. It is entirely silent.

The HoloSec Protocol

HoloSec operates on a strict "Radio Silence" protocol. The HoloSec engine is designed to run on devices with radios physically disabled or air-gapped. If you must transfer data, do it via wired connection (USB). Wireless convenience is the enemy of security.
