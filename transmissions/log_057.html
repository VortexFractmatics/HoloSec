How to Properly Use a Burner Phone

ID: 047 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

Rule 1: Cash Only

If you buy a burner phone with a credit card, it is linked to your identity. If you turn it on at your house, it is linked to your location. To have a true burner, you must buy it with cash, far from your home, and never bring it to your primary residence.

Rule 2: Battery Removal

A phone that is "off" is not off. The baseband processor often remains active. The only safe phone is one with the battery physically removed. Modern smartphones with sealed batteries are essentially tracking devices you cannot turn off.

The Data Courier

HoloSec can be used in conjunction with burner tech. Store your encrypted data on a microSD card. Insert it into the burner only when needed. Remove it instantly. If the phone is compromised or seized, the data is not on the internal storage.
