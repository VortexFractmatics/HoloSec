"Incognito Mode" Does Not Hide You From Your ISP

ID: 046 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The Local Illusion

Incognito Mode only stops your browser from saving history. It does NOT stop your Internet Service Provider (ISP) from seeing every site you visit. Verizon, Comcast, and AT&T log your DNS requests and sell that browsing history to advertisers.

DNS Leaks

Even if you use a VPN, if your computer is misconfigured, your DNS requests might still "leak" outside the tunnel, revealing your activity to the ISP. You are never as anonymous as you think.

The Offline Guarantee

The only true privacy is offline privacy. HoloSec vaults function without an internet connection. There is no DNS request to resolve. There is no IP address to log. When you open a file in HoloSec, the only witnesses are you and the machine.
