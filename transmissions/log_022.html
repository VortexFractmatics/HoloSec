The Human Patch: Beating Social Engineering

ID: 022 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

You Are the Bug

The most sophisticated firewall in the world cannot stop a hacker who calls you pretending to be IT support and asks for your password. This is "Social Engineering." It hacks the human, not the machine.

The Phishing Epidemic

90% of data breaches start with a phishing email. "Urgent: Invoice Overdue." "Security Alert: Login Detected." You click. You type. They win. If your data lives in the cloud, one mistake gives them everything.

The Offline Defense

HoloSec removes the vector entirely. Because your critical vault is offline, you cannot "log in" to it via a fake website. If a hacker steals your email password, they find... emails. They do not find your HoloSec vault, because it doesn't exist on the internet. You can be tricked, you can be phished, and your core secrets remain safe.
