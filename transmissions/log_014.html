The "Evil Maid" Attack: Who Touched Your Laptop?

ID: 014 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The Scenario

You are traveling for business. You leave your laptop in the hotel room safe. You go to dinner. While you are gone, a housekeeper (or someone dressed as one) enters the room, opens the safe with a master code, and inserts a USB drive into your laptop to install a keylogger.

HoloSec's Boot Integrity

This is the "Evil Maid" attack. HoloSec mitigates this by allowing you to run your entire secure environment from an external USB drive that you take with you to dinner. The laptop left in the room is just hardware. It has no data.

The "Tripwire" File

HoloSec also includes a "Tripwire" feature. You can place a dummy file on the laptop's desktop. If that file is accessed, modified, or moved while you are gone, HoloSec will alert you the next time you plug in your Master Key. You will know your perimeter was breached.
