Gone in 60 Seconds: The Relay Attack

ID: 037 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

Your Key Fob is Broadcasting

Modern cars unlock when you get close. This means your key fob is constantly shouting "I AM HERE!" Thieves use a $50 relay device to amplify this signal from inside your house to your driveway. The car thinks the key is next to the door. It unlocks. They drive away.

The Faraday Solution

Just like your HoloSec USB drive, your car keys need to be shielded. Store them in a Faraday box or pouch when you get home.

Digital Hygiene

This illustrates a core HoloSec principle: Signal Containment. Whether it's your car key or your encrypted data, if it is broadcasting, it is vulnerable. Keep your critical assets silent.
