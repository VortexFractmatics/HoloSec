Software "Mute" is a Lie

ID: 061 // CLEARANCE: RED

When you click the "Microphone Mute" icon in Zoom, Teams, or on your iPhone, you are not disconnecting the microphone.

You are sending a software request to the Operating System asking it to please stop processing the audio stream. The circuit remains closed. The power remains on. The diaphragm is still vibrating.

The Malware Bypass:
Sophisticated malware (like Pegasus) bypasses the UI layer entirely. It taps the audio driver directly. Your screen says "Muted." Your green light is off. But the hacker is listening to your conversation in real-time.
