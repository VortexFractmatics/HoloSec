The Data Broker Industry

Companies like Acxiom and Oracle possess a "shadow profile" of you. They know your credit score, your medical history, your GPS location history, and your purchases. They sell this to insurers, banks, and advertisers.

Starving the Beast

Every time you use Google Drive or Gmail, you feed the beast. They scan your data to build this profile.

The Dark Vault

HoloSec allows you to go "Dark." By moving your sensitive financial and personal data out of the cloud and into a local, encrypted vault, you stop feeding them. You create a black hole in their dataset. If enough people do this, their predictive models fail. Privacy is not just about protection; it is an act of rebellion.
