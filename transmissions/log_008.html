The Multi-Billion Dollar Leak

For engineers, architects, and designers, your CAD files and schematics are your lifeblood. Yet, standard industry practice is to store these on cloud servers (Dropbox, Autodesk Cloud) or local drives protected only by a Windows login.

This is negligence. Corporate espionage is rampant. If a competitor gains access to your unreleased prototype files, your patent leverage evaporates instantly.

Air-Gapped Sovereignty

HoloSec is designed to run in a fully air-gapped environment. You can install the engine on a dedicated USB drive and work on your sensitive blueprints on a machine that never touches the internet. The encryption keys are generated from mouse movements (Swarm Entropy), meaning they are truly random and not derived from an online server.

Client Handover Protocol

When you need to transport files to a client, you don't email them. You use HoloSec to create a "Shard Volume." You send the encrypted volume via one channel and the specific QR key via a physical courier. Even if the network is compromised, the files are useless without the physical key.
