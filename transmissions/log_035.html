ID: 035 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The Illusion of "Incognito"

You delete your cookies. You use Incognito Mode. You think you are invisible. You are wrong. Websites use "Canvas Fingerprinting." They ask your browser to render a tiny, invisible 3D shape. Because every graphics card renders it slightly differently, this creates a unique ID for your machine.

Hardware ID

This "Fingerprint" follows you across the web. They know it's you, even if you change IP addresses or delete cookies.

The Compartmentalization Strategy

HoloSec advocates for "Contextual Isolation." Do not perform sensitive research on the same machine you use for Facebook. Keep your sensitive data in an offline HoloSec vault that never interacts with a browser. If the browser can't see the file, the fingerprint doesn't matter.
