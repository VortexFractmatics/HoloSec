Hot Wallets vs. Cold Storage: Don't Get Burned

ID: 049 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The MetaMask Risk

If your crypto wallet is a browser extension (like MetaMask or Phantom), it is a "Hot Wallet." It is connected to the internet. If you click a malicious link or sign a bad contract, your funds can be drained instantly.

True Cold Storage

Ledgers and Trezors are good, but they are obvious. If a thief sees a Ledger, they know you have crypto. HoloSec offers "Plausible Deniability" Cold Storage. You store your seed phrase in a HoloSec volume. The volume looks like random noise. Even if your computer is seized, there is no evidence that you own any crypto at all.

The Ultimate Backup

Hardware wallets can fail or break. HoloSec allows you to create indestructible, encrypted digital backups of your seed phrases that you can store in multiple physical locations (USB drives) for redundancy.
