Your Boss is Reading Your DMs

ID: 045 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The Admin Export

Do you complain about your boss on Slack or Microsoft Teams? Stop. Enterprise administrators have an "Export" function that allows them to read all messages, including private DMs, without notifying you. Workplace chat is not private. It is company property.

BYOD Risk

If you use your personal phone for work (Bring Your Own Device) and install Mobile Device Management (MDM) software, your company may have the ability to remote wipe your entire phone—including your personal photos—if you quit.

The Separation of Church and State

HoloSec enforces strict separation. Never store personal credentials or sensitive documents on a work machine. Keep your personal life in a portable HoloSec vault that you plug in only when needed, and remove immediately. Do not let your employer own your digital identity.
