Voice Cloning is Here

The Problem with Face ID

Deepfake video calls are next. A CEO recently lost $25 million because he hopped on a Zoom call with a "CFO" who was actually an AI deepfake. You can no longer trust your eyes or ears.

The Challenge-Response Key

In a world where identity is fluid, you need a "Hard Key." HoloSec acts as that key. If you need to verify an order or a transfer, you don't use voice. You use a cryptographic signature generated by your offline vault. It is mathematical proof of identity that cannot be faked by a neural network.
