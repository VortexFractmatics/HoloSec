Virtual Machines Are Not Enough: The Case for Burner Hardware

ID: 040 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The VM Escape

Security pros love Virtual Machines (VMs). But sophisticated malware can perform a "VM Escape," breaking out of the virtual environment to infect the host OS. If your host machine is compromised, your VM is compromised.

Physical Separation

For truly Level 5 operations (whistleblowing, cold crypto storage), software separation is insufficient. You need physical separation. A "Burner" laptop. Bought with cash. Never connected to your home Wi-Fi. Used only for one purpose.

HoloSec Portability

HoloSec is designed to live on a USB stick, making it the perfect companion for burner hardware. You can plug it into a cheap $200 laptop, access your secrets, wipe the laptop's RAM, and walk away. The secrets stay on the stick, not the machine.
