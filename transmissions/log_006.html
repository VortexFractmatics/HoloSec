Decentralizing Trust: The 3-of-5 Rule

ID: 006 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The Binary Risk Factor

Security usually deals in absolutes. If you have the key, you have 100% access. If you lose the key, you have 0% access. This "Single Point of Failure" is unacceptable for high-net-worth individuals or enterprise storage.

Shamir's Secret Sharing (SSS)

HoloSec integrates a mathematical protocol called Shamir's Secret Sharing. It allows us to split the Master Key into "Shards." These are typically 5 distinct QR codes.

The Threshold: We set a recovery threshold of 3. This means:

  • Shard 1 + Shard 2 + Shard 3 = UNLOCK
  • Shard 1 + Shard 5 + Shard 4 = UNLOCK
  • Shard 1 + Shard 2 only = FAIL

Strategic Deployment

You can now decentralize your security physically. Put one shard in a bank box. Give one to your lawyer. Burry one in the yard. Keep one in your head. If your house burns down (destroying Shard 1), you are safe because you have the others. If a thief steals Shard 2, they are useless because they lack the others. It is the only way to be disaster-proof.
