Attorney-Client Privilege in the Digital Age

ID: 018 // CLEARANCE: BLACK_BOX

The Cloud is a Leak

Lawyers have an ethical duty to protect client information. Yet, many firms upload discovery files, evidence, and strategy memos to OneDrive or Dropbox. This is a breach waiting to happen. If the cloud provider is subpoenaed, your "Privilege" is tested against their Terms of Service.

The Digital Evidence Locker

HoloSec provides a compliant "Digital Evidence Locker." You can create a separate encrypted volume for each client case. These volumes are offline and protected by unique keys.

Chain of Custody

When you close a case, you don't just "delete" the files. You can archive the HoloSec volume to cold storage (DVD/USB) and hand it to the client. You retain no data, ensuring that if your firm is hacked in the future, that client's data is not there to be stolen. It is the ultimate form of liability reduction.
